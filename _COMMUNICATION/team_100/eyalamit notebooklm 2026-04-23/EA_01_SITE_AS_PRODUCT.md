# EyalAmit.co.il — The Site as a Product

## What This Site Is (and What It Is Not)

The EyalAmit.co.il website is a **conversion-first, authority-building, SEO-optimized digital home** for the didgeridoo treatment and breathwork center run by Eyal Amit in Pardes Hanna–Karkur, Israel. It is not a store, not an archive, not a general portfolio site.

The most important thing to understand about this project: the previous site was actually four different sites merged into one — a service site (didgeridoo lessons, breathwork, sound healing), a WooCommerce store, a book archive, and a historical performance and storytelling archive. All of these were tangled together with no clear hierarchy, unclear messaging, and no conversion architecture. The result was a site with ~81 content pages, 54 posts, 7 products, 50 QR pages, 24 irrelevant Portfolio pages, and 1,000+ media files — and a brand that was strong but could not communicate its core value clearly within the first few seconds of a visit.

The new site is a **clean slate rebuild** — not a renovation of the old site. The goal is not to preserve what existed. The goal is to build the site that the brand actually deserves: a professional center for breath treatment and didgeridoo work, with clear information hierarchy, clean UX, and SEO/AEO built in from day one.

---

## The Four Jobs of the New Site

Every design, content, and structural decision is evaluated against four primary functions:

**Job 1: Clarity** — within 5 seconds, any visitor should understand: *what* Eyal does (breath treatment via didgeridoo), *for whom*, and *how to start*. The old site failed this test. The new site is built to pass it.

**Job 2: Authority** — the site should read as a professional center, not a hobbyist, not a music teacher, not a general spiritual healer. Eyal has been working in this field since 1999. Maccabi health fund (Israel's major HMO) recognizes didgeridoo as a complementary medicine instrument for snoring and sleep apnea treatment. BMJ published a randomized controlled trial on the topic. The site should reflect this depth — not bury it under generic wellness copy.

**Job 3: Conversion** — every page has a clear next step. The site's commercial goal is not to sell products online (there is no internal checkout). The commercial goal is to generate qualified contacts: people who book a session, sign up for a workshop, or reach out to discuss a treatment process. Every page leads toward "לתיאום שיחת היכרות" (to schedule a discovery call).

**Job 4: Discovery** — the site should be found by people searching in Hebrew and English, by Google, by AI search tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini), and by local search. SEO, AEO (AI Engine Optimization), and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) are first-class requirements — not add-ons.

## What Makes This Site Different from a Standard Business Site

**The content is genuinely valuable.** Eyal has 25+ years of hands-on experience. He has a personal story that explains the method authentically. He has published three books. The treatment pages include real clinical research citations (BMJ RCT), real FAQ answers written from practice, and real testimonials. This is not generic wellness content — it has genuine informational depth that can perform well in both search and AI-assisted discovery.

**The method is specific and defensible.** The positioning is not "sound healing" (too broad), not "didgeridoo lessons" (too narrow), not "breathing exercises" (too generic). The specific claim is: **breath treatment via didgeridoo** — an active practice where the patient learns to work with their breath through the instrument. The branded method name is **cbDIDG** (circular breathing / didgeridoo). This is precise enough to be ownable and broad enough to encompass treatment, lessons, and sound healing under one roof.

**There is no competitor clutter.** The didgeridoo-for-health niche in Israel is very thin. There is no major competitor. The opportunity for the site to become the defining Israeli authority resource on the topic is real — but it requires the content depth and technical SEO execution to capitalize on the opportunity.

**AI search is a genuine growth channel.** When someone asks ChatGPT "what is didgeridoo therapy?" or "where can I learn didgeridoo in Israel?" the answer will come from content that AI systems have indexed and trust. The site is being built with explicit AEO/GEO optimization: structured Q&A format, schema markup, explicit permission for AI crawlers, and traceable content structure. This is not standard for Israeli service sites in 2026 — it is a competitive differentiation.

## What the Site Is NOT Building

These decisions are binding and documented:

- **No internal store or checkout.** Physical products (handmade instruments, accessories) and services link to external payment (Green Invoice / "בוקר" billing platform). No WooCommerce, no payment gateway, no cart.
- **No full archive.** Eyal's performance history, storytelling work, press from 15 years ago — these exist in a legacy section that is maintained (not deleted) but is not part of the main marketing tree. Visitors come to the main site to learn about what Eyal offers today.
- **No heavy page builder.** No Elementor, no Divi, no WPBakery. The build uses GeneratePress (parent) + `ea-eyalamit` (child theme) + ACF fields. The advantage: speed, maintainability, clean markup for SEO.
- **No competing SEO plugins.** One SEO plugin only: Yoast SEO. Two competing plugins create conflicting canonicals and sitemap issues.

## The Site's Competitive Opportunity

Israel's organic health and wellness web landscape is generally underdeveloped in terms of modern SEO and AI-readiness. Most practitioners in the breathwork / sound healing space have:
- Outdated sites
- Thin, narrative-only content (no FAQ, no structured Q&A)
- No schema markup
- No AI crawler permissions
- No English presence

The EyalAmit.co.il rebuild, done correctly, can become the definitive authority source for "didgeridoo therapy," "circular breathing treatment," and related queries in the Hebrew-speaking market — and for the English-speaking market that searches for Israeli didgeridoo instruction and sound healing.

The site is not chasing keywords. It is building an entity: **Eyal Amit** + **cbDIDG method** + **Pardes Hanna breathwork center** = an identifiable, authoritative, specific entity in the didgeridoo-for-health domain.
