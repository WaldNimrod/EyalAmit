### Composition decisions
1. **Pullquote** is NOT a new atom — it is a token-level composition (D-14 type weight-200/1.4rem + `--ea-terracotta` inline-start border). If team_80 deems a standalone `atom-content-pullquote` warranted, that is a GCR, not a unilateral change. **(Open question Q1.)**
2. **Media gallery** reuses `atom-content-book-gallery` rather than inventing an editorial gallery — same grid math (16:7 hero + 4:3 tiles).
3. **Related slot** reuses `atom-service-tile` (not a bespoke "editorial card") — also serves as the /press clip-row primitive, keeping one card vocabulary across the cluster.
4. Motion left static so Eyal judges composition, not animation (mirrors the reduced-motion disable block).
