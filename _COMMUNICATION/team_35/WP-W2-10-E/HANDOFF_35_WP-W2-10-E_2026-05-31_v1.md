2. **One detail vertical for book + product.** book-detail and shop-item share hero→prose→gallery→FAQ→purchase/closing. Difference is CTA semantics only (book = external buy link; product = contact, per B02 matrix).
3. **Hero is asset-free** (D-14 gradient), so the hero is NOT a blocker — only card/gallery imagery is asset-gated.
4. **Purchase pill repeated 3× on detail** (hero, mid, closing) per the deployed contract — conversion-first.
