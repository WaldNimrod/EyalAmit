## 4. Composition decisions
1. **Archive grid** 3→2→1 columns (D-14 breakpoints 900/560px). Cards lead with title + clean excerpt; category eyebrow + date framing.
2. **Filter** as horizontal wrapping chip row (centred); active chip = accent border + bg-alt. Mirrors the deployed `.ea-blog-filter` exactly.
3. **Pagination** chip row, numbered, current = accent, `aria-current`. 5 pages reflected.
4. **Single reading typography (AC-D3):** `--ea-type-body-lg` at `line-height:1.9`, measure capped at **66ch** for comfortable RTL line-length. Generous `--ea-space-3` paragraph rhythm.
5. **Featured image:** gradient placeholder (post has none) carrying descriptive `aria-label`; not a blocker.
6. **Share + Related** added as flagged composition *proposals* (spec mandates them for single, but they are absent from the W2-06 build). Marked with an on-screen "הצעת קומפוזיציה" badge so Eyal sees they are net-new.
