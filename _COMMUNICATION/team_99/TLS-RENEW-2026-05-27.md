## 2. Two compounding problems

1. **Hostname mismatch (by uPress design):**
   The cert covers `*.upress.io` and `upress.io`. The staging hostname is `eyalamit-co-il-2026.s887.upress.link` — a different parent domain (`.upress.link` ≠ `.upress.io`). A wildcard `*.upress.io` does not match any `.upress.link` subdomain. uPress's staging sandbox routes `.upress.link` hostnames through their default infrastructure that presents the platform's own cert; per-sandbox certs are not provisioned.
   - This is confirmed by `local/.env.upress` line 11: "אין תעודת SSL ציבורית תקינה כמו בפרודקשן [...] השתמשו ב־**http://** על אותו hostname [...] `https://` עלול להציג אזהרת תעודה / שגיאת curl".

2. **Expired cert (Nov 2025):**
   Even if a client trusted the wrong-CN cert, it's been expired for ~6 months. This is uPress's responsibility — their internal `*.upress.io` wildcard has not been rotated.

## 3. Why team_99 cannot renew

- ❌ **No certbot path:** the 443 listener is uPress's nginx, not under our control. We cannot install a Let's Encrypt cert on the staging hostname; uPress's proxy would terminate TLS with its own cert anyway.
- ❌ **No uPress panel SSO from CLI:** the uPress control panel (`https://s-il-887-tbh.upress.io/...`) requires interactive human login. team_99 (CLI agent) has no credentials/SSO for it. The mandate's "uPress panel (preferred)" option requires a human operator.
- ❌ **No Cloudflare in front of staging hostname:** the staging domain is not routed through Cloudflare; we cannot use Cloudflare Flexible SSL.
- ❌ **Not addressable via wp-cli or WP-admin:** TLS is below the WordPress application layer.

## 4. Recommended path (escalation to team_00)

The cert situation is **uPress-side, by-design, staging-only**. Recommended options ordered by effort:

1. **Accept (recommended):** keep staging on HTTP. Production at `https://eyalamit.co.il` (M7 cutover) gets its own legitimate cert via uPress's production provisioning. The mandate's §1 Task C note already authorises this path.
2. **Open uPress support ticket** to request:
   (a) rotation of their expired `*.upress.io` wildcard, and
   (b) clarification on whether per-sandbox `.upress.link` certs can be issued. Requires human / team_00 action; not blocking R5 re-QA.
3. **Add SNI alias / custom hostname** on staging via uPress panel (e.g., `staging.eyalamit.co.il`) and let Let's Encrypt issue against that. Requires human / team_00 action; not blocking R5 re-QA.
