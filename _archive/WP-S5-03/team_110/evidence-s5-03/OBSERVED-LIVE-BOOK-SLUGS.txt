🔴 SEPARATE, GENUINE DEFECT FOUND WHILE CHECKING THIS — a LIVE BROKEN LINK on a QR page
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
/qr/qr2/ contains:

    <a href="/books/%D7%A6%D7%91%D7%A2-%D7%91%D7%9B%D7%97%D7%95%D7%9C-%D7%95%D7%96%D7%A8%D7%95%D7%A7-%D7%9C%D7%99%D7%9D/"></a>
    = /books/צבע-בכחול-וזרוק-לים/   -> HTTP 404

There are TWO anchors to that same 404 on /qr/qr2/:
  1. <a href="/books/צבע-בכחול-וזרוק-לים/"></a>            — EMPTY text. This is the `link-name`
     serious axe violation recorded in WP-S5-06's AC-4 baseline
     (evidence/s5-06/a11y-rtl/AC-4-AXE-BASELINE-FINDING.txt). At the time it was classified as
     "pre-existing and out of scope" — correct for that WP's contract, but UNDERSTATED: it is
     not merely an a11y nit, it is a dead link.
  2. <a href="/books/צבע-בכחול-וזרוק-לים/">לדף הספר&gt;&gt;</a>  — a VISIBLE, clickable link
     reading "to the book page >>". A reader who scans the QR printed in Eyal's book, sees the
     book reference and clicks it, lands on a Not Found. The correct target is
     /books/tsva-bekahol/ (verified 200).

BOUNDED SCOPE (measured, not estimated) — grep over the whole QR seed:
  distinct hebrew-slug /books/ targets in the seed : 1  (/books/צבע-בכחול-וזרוק-לים/)
  anchors pointing at it                           : 2
  QR pages affected                                : 1  (qr2 only)
  => a small, contained fix: repoint both anchors to /books/tsva-bekahol/ in
     ea-w2-07-qr-content-data.php, then reseed with a new self-guarded -once flag (the same
     mechanism WP-S5-07 §4.F used). No other QR page carries a hebrew-slug /books/ link.

SCOPE: this is NOT WP-S5-06 (facade), NOT WP-S5-07 (NAP), and NOT WP-S5-03 (whose scope is the
GSC legacy-URL 301 map — /books/צבע.../ is not a legacy GSC URL, it is a CURRENT internal link
in the QR seed content, ea-w2-07-qr-content-data.php). Fixing it means editing the QR seed +
reseeding — the same mechanism WP-S5-07 §4.F used.

ROUTED: to team_100 as a new finding, for a content/hygiene WP — together with the same
sweep for any other internal /books/<hebrew-slug>/ links in the QR seed. It does not block
WP-S5-05, but Eyal's readers hit it today.
