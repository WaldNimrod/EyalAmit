Didgeridoo healing center - Eyal Amit

Pardes Hanna–Karkur area, Israel · 25+ years of teaching and therapeutic work

Eyal Amit’s center brings together didgeridoo playing, circular breathing, and sound healing in one calm, professional setting. Whether you want to learn an instrument, explore breath-based relaxation, or experience guided sound meditation, sessions are built around clear boundaries, respect for the client, and decades of field experience.

What we offer

  • Lessons & playing — Didgeridoo instruction for different levels, from first sounds to more advanced technique.
  • Therapy & breath — Breath-centered sessions using the didgeridoo as a tool for regulation and presence (not a substitute for medical care).
  • Sound healing — Sound-based meditation and listening experiences built around the didgeridoo.
  • Instruments — Handmade and adjusted instruments, repairs, and accessories; purchases via secure external payment (same flow as the Hebrew site).
  • Lectures & workshops — Formats for groups, organizations, and intensive days (by arrangement).
  • Courses — Digital and structured programs; enrollment via the Courses section (external platform, as on the Hebrew site).
